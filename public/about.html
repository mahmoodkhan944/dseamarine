D Sea Marine is a UAE-based maritime services and marine trading company headquartered in Ajman. We deliver technical marine solutions to ship owners, vessel operators, offshore contractors, and marine service providers operating in regional and international waters.

Our scope of services includes maritime consultancy, vessel chartering, marine equipment and machinery trading, bunker supply, sea cargo logistics, and vessel and crew certification support.

D Sea Marine operates in accordance with applicable international maritime conventions, classification society requirements, and industry best practices, ensuring operational compliance, safety, and efficiency.

Vision

To establish D Sea Marine as a technically competent and trusted marine
service provider within the regional and global maritime sector, recognized for
compliance-driven operations, quality assurance, and long-term client partnerships.

Mission

To provide technically sound maritime services and marine trading solutions
that ensure regulatory compliance, operational reliability, and safety across
all vessel types and marine operations.

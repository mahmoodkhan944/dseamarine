Life Saving Equipment Trading

Supply of SOLAS-compliant life-saving appliances and safety equipment, including lifeboats, liferafts, life jackets, fire-fighting systems, and associated safety gear, in accordance with international maritime safety regulations.

Services / Life Saving Equipment Trading

D Sea Marine specializes in the supply and trading of certified life-saving appliances (LSA) and safety equipment designed to meet stringent international maritime safety standards. Our product range complies with the SOLAS (Safety of Life at Sea) Convention, IMO regulations, and classification society requirements to ensure maximum safety and regulatory compliance on board vessels and offshore installations.

Marine Safety & Life-Saving Equipment

Lifeboats & Liferafts

Inflatable and rigid lifeboats and liferafts equipped with certified launching systems and complete survival kits.

Personal Flotation Devices

Life jackets, immersion suits, and rescue harnesses designed for crew safety and emergency response.

Fire-Fighting Equipment

Portable fire extinguishers, fixed fire suppression systems, and fire detection and alarm solutions.

Emergency Communication Devices

EPIRBs, SARTs, and maritime survival communication equipment for rapid distress signaling and rescue coordination.

Safety Signage & Escape Systems

Safety signage, escape route lighting, and immersion solutions designed for fast evacuation and crew protection.

All products are sourced from certified manufacturers and undergo rigorous quality assurance checks to comply with international statutory and class requirements. We provide consultancy on equipment selection, certification, installation guidelines, and maintenance schedules to ensure operational readiness and regulatory compliance.

For technical inquiries, operational support, or marine service coordination, please contact our team.

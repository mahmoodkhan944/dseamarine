Maritime Consultancy

Provision of technical maritime consultancy covering vessel operations, regulatory compliance, safety management systems, and operational planning. Our consultancy services support ship owners and operators in meeting flag state, port state, and classification society requirements.

Services / Maritime Consultancy

D Sea Marine is specialized in the provision of professional consultancy services across a broad range of maritime disciplines.

Maritime Consultancy Services

Shipbuilding & Ship Repair Advisory

Advisory support on shipbuilding and ship repair contracts including technical specifications, supervision, contract terms, and compliance with class and statutory requirements.

Vessel Sale & Purchase (S&P)

Comprehensive consultancy for vessel sale and purchase transactions covering valuation, due diligence, inspections, contract negotiation, and delivery procedures.

Chartering & Operational Advisory

Advisory services related to vessel operations and chartering arrangements including Bareboat, Time Charter, and Voyage Charter agreements.

Vessel Inspections & Surveys

Vessel inspections, condition surveys, and compliance assessments to verify technical condition, safety standards, and regulatory adherence.

Flag State & Certification Support

Advisory support for vessel registration, flag state matters, and the issuance and renewal of statutory and class certificates.

Maritime Claims & Pollution Advisory

Consultancy on marine debts, maritime liens, vessel arrests and releases, pollution incidents, environmental damage assessments, and related claims under international conventions.

For technical inquiries, operational support, or marine service coordination, please contact our team.

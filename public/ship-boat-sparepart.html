Ship & Boat Spare Parts and Components

Supply and trading of certified marine spare parts and components for various vessel types. All parts are sourced from approved manufacturers and suppliers.

Services / Ship & Boat Spare Parts and Components

We specialize in the comprehensive resale and supply of spare parts and components critical to the repair, maintenance, overhaul, and operational upgrading of vessels and offshore installations. Our product portfolio includes, but is not limited to:

Marine Machinery & Systems

Main & Auxiliary Engines

Main propulsion and auxiliary engines for marine and offshore applications, compliant with OEM specifications and classification society requirements.

Boilers & Pressure Vessels

Marine boilers and pressure vessels engineered for safety, operational efficiency, and full regulatory compliance.

Shafting & Power Transmission

Shafting systems, spindles, couplings, and mechanical components essential for reliable propulsion and power transmission.

Piping, Valves & Fittings

Certified piping, valves, flanges, and fittings for marine and offshore environments supporting ballast, fluid transfer, and process systems.

Navigation & Communication Systems

Marine-grade radar, VHF/UHF radios, satellite communications, and integrated bridge systems for safe navigation.

Instrumentation & Monitoring

Measuring and monitoring instruments for engines, environmental conditions, and safety systems compliant with IMO and SOLAS standards.

All components supplied undergo strict quality assurance processes, sourced exclusively from certified OEMs and approved aftermarket manufacturers. This guarantees compatibility with vessel and offshore asset class notations and flag state statutory requirements, ensuring optimal reliability and operational integrity in harsh marine environments.
Our supply chain management supports timely delivery, tailored inventory solutions, and logistical coordination to minimize downtime during critical maintenance or emergency repair operations. We also provide technical advisory support to assist clients in component selection, retrofit planning, and lifecycle cost optimization.

For technical inquiries, operational support, or marine service coordination, please contact our team.

Sea Cargo Services

Provision of sea freight and marine logistics services for bulk cargo, containerized shipments, and project cargo, with professional handling, route planning, and compliance with maritime transport regulations.

Services / Sea Cargo Services

Sea Cargo Transportation & Marine Logistics

Sea Cargo Transportation

End-to-end sea cargo transportation services supporting bulk, containerized (FCL/LCL), breakbulk, heavy lift, and project cargo across domestic, regional, and international trade routes.

Voyage Planning & Chartering

Comprehensive voyage planning, vessel nomination, and chartering support aligned with cargo requirements, schedules, and operational constraints.

Cargo Handling & Securing

Cargo suitability assessments, loading and discharge coordination, stowage planning, lashing, and securing in accordance with CSM, class, and industry best practices.

Shipping Documentation & Compliance

Management of bills of lading, manifests, certificates of origin, and customs documentation ensuring compliance with Incoterms, SOLAS, MARPOL, and IMO regulations.

Project & Heavy Lift Logistics

Engineering-led logistics solutions including route surveys, lifting studies, risk assessments, and execution planning for complex and oversized cargo.

Operational Oversight & Coordination

Continuous operational monitoring, schedule control, and coordination with terminals, stevedores, surveyors, and port agents to ensure timely and reliable delivery.

For technical inquiries, operational support, or marine service coordination, please contact our team.
